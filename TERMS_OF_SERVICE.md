## 2. Description of Service

SpeakSummarize is an AI-powered voice recording and transcription application featuring Echo, your calm AI memory companion, that:
- Records audio on your device with real-time waveform visualization
- Transcribes recordings using OpenAI Whisper with speaker diarization
- Generates structured AI summaries with key insights, action items, and topics
- Provides AI chat functionality with Echo using Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Enables semantic search across all your recordings
- Supports multiple independent chat conversations
- Exports summaries as beautifully formatted PDFs
